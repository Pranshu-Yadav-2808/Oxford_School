About Oxford

Oxford International School is a place where academic excellence meets strong values. We provide a nurturing and disciplined environment that inspires students to learn, grow, and succeed with confidence. Our experienced and dedicated teachers focus on concept-based learning, critical thinking, and overall personality development of every child. With modern infrastructure, smart classrooms, and a safe campus, we ensure a balanced educational experience that prepares students for future challenges while instilling moral values, leadership qualities, and a lifelong love for learning.

Our Vision

To nurture responsible, confident and future-ready students.

Our Mission

Providing quality education with strong moral and ethical values.

Our Values

Discipline, Integrity, Excellence and Respect.

Message from the Principal

At Oxford International School, we believe that education is not limited to academics alone. Our goal is to nurture confident, disciplined, and responsible individuals who are prepared to face future challenges with integrity and determination. We focus on holistic development by encouraging curiosity, creativity, leadership, and strong moral values in every student.

We strongly believe in a collaborative partnership between parents and teachers, ensuring that each child receives individual attention, care, and guidance. Together, we strive to create a safe, supportive, and inspiring learning environment where students can discover their true potential and grow into successful lifelong learners.

— Mrs. Rooma Tiwari
